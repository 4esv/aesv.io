## (Silence)

The door was locked, the house was quiet and the last light, this light, was dimmed. Everyone had been thought of, all errands run and responsibilities handled. It was time to lose track of time.

After a long time of making time and wasting time as if time were perpetually bound to be had in the wrong amount, could you blame him? He wouldn't blame himself, nor would he let your blame deter him. He's just winding down, like anyone, like everyone.

_Silence_

## _(Creak)_

Having wound down, it was time to call it a night. With no one to say goodnight to, he carries his peace down the hallway past empty rooms preceding a less empty one at the end of the hall.

_Creak_

Jolted him like cold water. Unbeknownst to him, this was one of those situations where one's imagination finds itself unfortunately in tune with reality. Downstairs, a two-time felon was having a look around _inside_. Presuming himself, without particular worry, to be in a vacant house.

## _(Click)_

Back upstairs, wound up, the journey is made in reverse. Hauling his stress across the hall back to his study. Instead of winding down, he was merely getting the instrument; rather than the usual one, the occasion called for all six. Just in case.

With three pounds of courage in his shaky hand, down the stairs he went. Pounding heart and straining eyes, a corner is turned and a silhouette appears. As soon as he clocks it he pulls the trigger.

_Click_

## _(Bang)_

The coldest of shivers rattled his spine as if his marrow was being flushed with water from the river. Help was all he could think of but not so much as a whimper made it out of him.

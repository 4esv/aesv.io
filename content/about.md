Half-Mexican, half-American, all generalist. I grew up between two countries and a handful of hobbies, never quite picked one. Day job is integration and automation work; the [receipts are at /work](/work). The rest of the week splits between bands, books, broken cars, and fire-company calls.

The unifying thread, paid or otherwise, is connection. Get two things to talk to each other well enough that you stop thinking about the wiring. Same job whether the two things are computers, musicians, or a stranger and dispatch.
